Medusa

Medusa is one of the Gorgon sisters in Greek mythology. Her hair consists of snakes, and she has the power to turn anyone who looks directly into her eyes into stone. She was originally a beautiful woman, but was cursed by the goddess Athena and transformed into this terrifying form.

Today, Medusa is frequently featured in art and literature as a symbol of both fear and protection. She remains one of the most recognized figures in mythology.
